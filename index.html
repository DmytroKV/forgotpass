How to Reset Your Password?

1. Go to the bottom of the page.

2. Find Reset password button.

3. Click the button.

4. And a box will appear, enter your message below and click "Send Message".

How it works.

We will get the message and see your email, we'll use that email to reset your password.

After we reset your password, we'll message you back with the new password.
